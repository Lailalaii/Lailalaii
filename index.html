Rosa Parks

The mother of the freedom movement

Here's a time line of Mrs. Park's life:

  • 1913 - Born Febuary 4 in Tuskegee, Alabama
  • 1932 - Marries Raymond Parks
  • 1943 - First incident on segregated bus. Joins NAACP and becomes active in Civil Rights Movement
  • 1955 - Montgomery Bus Boycott(Dec 1 1955 to Dec 20 1956). Refuses to Give Up Seat on Bus
  • 1979 - NAACP awards Rosa Parks the Spingarn Medal
  • 1992 - My Story an autobiography is published
  • 1994 - Be attacked in her appartment
  • 1996 - Bill Clinton awards Rosa Parks the Presidential Medal of Freedom
  • 2005 - Dies at the age of 92
